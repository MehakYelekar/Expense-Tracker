About

Expense Tracker is your personal finance companion, designed to help you manage your money with ease. Our mission is to empower users to take control of their finances, set budgets, and achieve their savings goals.

  • Track daily expenses and categorize them easily
  • Visualize your spending with interactive graphs
  • Set and monitor monthly budgets
  • Access your data securely from anywhere

Whether you're a student, professional, or entrepreneur, Expense Tracker is built for everyone who wants to make smarter financial decisions.
